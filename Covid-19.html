Insights into the Unprecedented Crisis

Examining COVID-19's Global Reach: A Comprehensive Analysis of Cases, Trends, and Future Projections

The Aim of the Project

The aim of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of COVID-19, encompassing cases, trends, and future projections. By examining a wide range of data sources and employing advanced analytical techniques, the project seeks to gain valuable insights into the global footprint of the pandemic.

Stages of the Project: Collect, Process & Visualize

I extracted and analyzed raw data points using SQL in Microsoft SSMS to uncover insights into the global impact of COVID-19. Through meticulous data cleaning and processing, I prepared the dataset for analysis. Leveraging advanced analytical techniques, I identified trends, patterns, and correlations within the data. To enhance comprehension, I visualized the processed data points using suitable tools. Lastly, I generated a forecast to provide valuable projections for future outcomes. This project empowered me to contribute actionable insights for effective planning and response strategies in the face of the pandemic.

"Data Source: Our World In Data"

The End Results

Using advanced data analysis techniques, I conducted an extensive examination of the impact of COVID-19 across continents. This comprehensive project delved into the effects of the pandemic on various regions, including a detailed analysis of its implications for Canada, United States, Sweden, and India. The findings from this in-depth analysis empowered global and regional entities to devise effective strategies and responses.
