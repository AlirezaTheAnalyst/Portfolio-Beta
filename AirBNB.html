Airbnb Price Point Analysis in Vancouver

Seasonal Insights: Decoding Vancouver's Airbnb Seasonal Trends & Price Fluctuations

The Aim of the Project

The aim of this project is to uncover seasonal insights through a comprehensive Airbnb price analysis in Vancouver. By examining data patterns and trends, the project seeks to provide valuable information about price variations across different seasons and locations within the city. The ultimate goal is to equip both travelers and hosts with knowledge that can aid in making informed decisions.

Stages of the Project: Collect, Process & Visualize

In this project, raw data points were extracted and meticulously processed using MySQL to rectify formatting issues and clean the data. By employing various techniques and SQL queries, the dataset was refined and prepared for further analysis. Leveraging the power of MySQL, the data points were analyzed to extract meaningful insights and uncover hidden patterns. Subsequently, Tableau was utilized to create interactive visualizations, enhancing the understanding and presentation of the data. This combination of MySQL data processing and analysis, followed by Tableau visualization, ensured a comprehensive exploration of the dataset, providing an engaging and informative experience for users.
